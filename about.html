About Us

CHANGAMKA SPARES LTD

The company started in 1998 and has grown tremendously to become one of the leading motor vehicle parts distributor and seller in Kenya. The growth is attributed to the fact that the company has a policy of dealing with quality products. As a result we have received repeated businesses from satisfied customers. The company believes that cheap is expensive in the long run.

Changamka Spares Ltd. is a leading automative aftermarket parts provider that serves both professional and do it yourself customers. Changamka Spares Ltd. operates stores and branches across the entire nation of Kenya.

Our main branches are loacted in our major cities i.e. Nairobi, Mombasa, Kisumu and minor branches are: Nakuru, Eldoret, Machakos, Kiambu, Voi e.t.c

Our mission is to become the leader in Motor vehicle spare parts in the country.
